Why Cilj?

Definerati si cilj. Kaj je vaš cilj?

Cilj
/t͡síːl/ (tseal)
Goal, target (Slovene)
Definirati
/definǐːˈrati/ (de-fin-i-ra-ti)
Define (Slovene)
Some Uses:
  • Definerati si cilj - Define your goal.
  • Določite cilj - Set a goal.
  • Kaj je vaš cilj? - What is your goal?
  • Lahko določite svoj cilj? - Can you define your goal?
  • Kaj si prizadevate doseči? - What are you trying to achieve?
  • Cilj si postavite visoko - Set your goal high.
  • Lahko si postavite svoj cilj. - You can set your own goal.
  • Zastavite si cilj visoko in ne ustavite, dokler ga ne dosežete. - Set a goal high and don’t stop until you reach it.
